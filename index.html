Personal Golf Club Design

Summary

Engineered and prototyped a custom face-balanced putter and toe-weighted 7-iron in Fusion 360, milling the putter head from aluminum and steel at the Purdue Design and Innovation Center.

Problem

The zero torque putter has recently become the center attraction for golfers. These putters center the shaft at the putter heads center of gravity to resist natural twisting and turning. These putters are typically expensive and dont offer specific alignment aids.

Inspiration

Customizing aspects of a putter head can allow the user to tailor there club to what they like. By modifying the following you can achieve different effects: alignment aids, shape, shaft location, surface angle, weight distribution, and the weight of the putter head.

Solution

To make the design easy to configure, variable surfaces make use of parametrics. The standard surface angle is tilted at 4 degrees to minimize spin and help the ball roll smoothly. There is a horizontal visual aid to help the user keep the putter on plane. The center of gravity is positioned 0.25 in behind the shaft, this is known as face balanced, and it helps keep the putter square.

Quick storage field raiser

Summary

Designed and fabricated a portable field-raising system to improve storage and setup of robotics tournament fields.

Problem

Vex Robotics competition rules states that competition fields must be raised two feet of the floor. This typically measns using and storing stage risers. These are bulky and take upwards of two hours to set up just one of them. This slows down the set up process and makes storing them difficult.

Inspiration

By creating a field raiser that can store not only itself but the field that sits on it would maximize setup time and make storage easiezar.

Solution

Used Fusion 360 to CAD the initial design, purchasing parts from McMaster and Home Depot while keeping a strict budget. The design hinged at the center point of the field and could be folded upwards for storage. To setup the field three locks would need to be unclipped and the field slowly unfolds.
