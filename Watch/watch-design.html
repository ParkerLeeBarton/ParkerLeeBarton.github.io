Watch Design Project | January 2026 - Present

Version 1

Figure 1: Version 1 Render

I’m designing and building a fully custom wristwatch, handling everything from CAD modeling to component selection. The plan is to cast the case in aluminum using lost-wax casting, exploring precision tolerances, surface finishes, and small-scale mechanical design. The case is modeled to fit all chosen movement components while balancing function and aesthetics.

My first casting attempt had a rough mold finish, so I’m waiting on new SLA wax from Bectal to try again. Even with this setback, the project has provided hands-on experience in design iteration, material selection, and process planning. When finished, the watch will be a fully functional, precision-engineered timepiece reflecting both technical skill and personal design.
